Puzzle 1/12: Crossroads

Down

  • 1. The trains in town travel on this.
  • 2. The name of the people to whom this land historically belonged.

Across

  • 2. The name of the creek that runs through Davis.

Right at the start your journey, you hit a crossroad, and a hawk appears. The hawk tells you that in order to keep going, you need to answer three questions. It then asks you all the questions at once and disappears, leaving you to puzzle them through.

Puzzle 2/12: Time

Davis wast inc'rp'rat'd in 1917. 2 sc’re annum and one less than a decade past, the town wast did found. what is yond date?

Insuper, a decade past 1917, ‘t did get its nameth sh'rten'd, doing hence with a quarter of a score characters. what wast the 'riginal nameth?

The hawk tells you a rhyme that leaves you baffled. It then tells you that there are 2 answers, without which you cannot move forward. You see grooves forming in the soil in front of you, forming the rhymes that the hawk spoke.

Puzzle 3/12: Birds

The hawk sits in front of you, eyes glimmering and head tilted. In front of the hawk is a scattered collection of pebbles. After some silence, the hawk tells you to identify shape what the pebbles form. You try to step forward, but there is something preventing you from moving.

Puzzle 4/12: Pathways

Ahead of you, you see a large number of paths going every which way. You try to move forward by picking a random path, but the paths scramble. The hawk appears again, and tells you that you need to focus on the town you started from before you continue.
